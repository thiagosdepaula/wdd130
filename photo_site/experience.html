The Portrait Experience

Step One: Inquire

First, you will fill out a quick contact form lets me know more about the session you are hoping for. Then I’ll send you my investment guide as well as a link for a consultation call. This call will be a no-pressure, casual conversation where I can explain my process to you. This will also be the opportunity to ask all of your questions to see if I am a good fit for you! If you decide that you would like to work with me, we’ll schedule a date for your session and sign your booking proposal.

Step Two: Prepare

Once we make things official, we will prepare for your session will a stying appointment. I will help you choose clothing for your session that photographs beautifully and makes you feel like a million bucks. These clothing items may be picked from your own closet, purchased from your favorite brands, or borrowed from my client closet. We’ll also decide together the best location for your session to take place, whether that be my studio or an outdoor location. At the end of this appointment, we will take a look at your home and decide the best place to display your artwork.

Step Three: Create

On the day of your session, my hair and make-up artist will meet you at my studio or at your house to make you feel beautiful and dolled-up. Then, we will continue to the portrait location where I will capture the sweet bond between you and your babies.

Step Four: Design and Order

Well meet once again to choose your favorite images from the session. I’ll create mockups of wall galleries and photo albums for you to envision in your home. From here, you’ll place your order and await your final delivery.

Step Five: Install and Enjoy

The artwork of your sweet family will be ready to be picked up approximately 8-12 weeks after your ordering appointment. I offer a complimentary installation for all of the framed prints in your gallery. After your prints are hung on your wall, you can enjoy them for years and remember how little your babies once were!
